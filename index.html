How To Plant A Tree

In Four Easy Steps

Step 1: Dig a Hole

Dig a hole that is as deep as the root ball and three times as wide.

If your dirt is clay-like, amend the soil with compost for better results.

Step 2: Put the Tree in the Hole.

Gently loosen the root ball of the tree. Be careful! Don't break them!

Set the top of the root ball at the same height as the top of the hole.

Step 3: Fill Hole with Dirt.

If your dirt is too dense (like clay), mix in compost so it will drain more easily.

Fill the hole with dirt and gently tap it down to eliminate air holes.

Step 4: Water and Mulch.

Build a circular dirt 'dam' a few inches high around your tree. Water thoroughly inside the circle.

Add mulch to help retain moisture at the roots. Be sure to water regularly until the tree is established!
